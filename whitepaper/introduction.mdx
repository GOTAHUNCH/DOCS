## The Problem

Prediction markets have demonstrated remarkable accuracy in forecasting events, often outperforming traditional polling and expert analysis. Yet current platforms remain inaccessible to mainstream users due to:

- **Complex Interfaces**: Order books, limit orders, and trading mechanics intimidate casual users
- **Wallet Friction**: Seed phrases, browser extensions, and manual gas management
- **Desktop Focus**: Poor or nonexistent mobile experiences
- **Isolated Experience**: No social features, community, or network effects

## The Solution

Hunch makes prediction markets as accessible as social media through:

- **Swipe Interface**: Simple YES/NO swipes on prediction cards
- **Invisible Blockchain**: Users never see wallets, gas, or transactions
- **Social Layer**: Follow predictors, share bets, compete on leaderboards
- **Mobile-First**: Built for the 5 billion smartphone users worldwide
