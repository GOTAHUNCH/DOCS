# Product Vision

Hunch envisions a world where predicting the future is as natural as scrolling social media. Our platform transforms prediction markets from a niche financial tool into a mainstream social experience.

## Core Experience: Swipe-to-Predict

The Hunch interface centers on an infinitely scrollable feed of prediction cards. Users encounter questions about politics, sports, crypto, entertainment, and current events.

<Steps>
  <Step title="See a Question">
    "Will Bitcoin hit $100K by December?"
  </Step>
  <Step title="Swipe Right for YES">
    Confident it will happen
  </Step>
  <Step title="Swipe Left for NO">
    Betting against it
  </Step>
  <Step title="Swipe Up to Skip">
    Not interested, next card
  </Step>
</Steps>

This mechanic reduces the cognitive load of prediction markets to a single intuitive gesture.

## User-Created Markets

Beyond curated markets from major data sources, Hunch enables users to create their own prediction markets:

| Market Type | Description | Resolution |
|-------------|-------------|------------|
| Personal | "Will I finish this book by Friday?" | Creator resolves |
| Community | Crowd-sourced questions | Community verification |
| Creator | Influencer-branded predictions | Verified resolution |

<Info>
User-created markets drive engagement, personalization, and viral growth through social sharing.
</Info>

## Live & In-Play Betting

Real-time prediction markets for live events represent a massive opportunity:

- **Sports**: In-game predictions with dynamic odds
- **Entertainment**: Live show outcomes, award ceremonies
- **Crypto**: Real-time price movement predictions
- **News**: Breaking news outcome predictions

Live markets drive engagement intensity and session frequency, creating habitual usage patterns.
