# Tokenomics

## Swipe Mining

Users earn $HUNCH regardless of bet outcomes through engagement:

| Action | Reward |
|--------|--------|
| Daily login | 10 $HUNCH |
| Place a bet | 5 $HUNCH |
| Win a bet | 100 $HUNCH bonus |
| Refer a friend | 1,000 $HUNCH |
| Create a market | 50 $HUNCH |

<Note>
Swipe mining rewards come from the Ecosystem & Rewards allocation and decrease over time to maintain sustainability.
</Note>
