# Go-to-Market Strategy

## Distribution Philosophy

Hunch employs a **crypto-global distribution strategy**, reaching users through crypto-native channels that transcend traditional geographic boundaries. This approach leverages the existing infrastructure of crypto communities to achieve rapid, cost-effective user acquisition.

## Geographic Strategy

### Target Markets

| Priority | Markets |
|----------|---------|

| **Secondary** | Brazil, Argentina, Thailand, South Africa |

## Legal Structure

Hunch operates through a **BVI Foundation** structure, providing the flexibility required for global crypto operations while maintaining appropriate legal protections.

| Entity | Purpose |
|--------|---------|
| BVI Foundation | Technology development and token issuance |
| Operating Subsidiary | Day-to-day operations |
| IP Holding | Intellectual property protection |
