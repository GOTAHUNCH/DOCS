# Executive Summary

Hunch is a mobile-first prediction market application that transforms complex betting mechanics into an intuitive, TikTok-style swipe interface. Built on Base (Coinbase L2), Hunch enables users to bet on real-world events by simply swiping YES or NO on prediction cards.

## Market Opportunity

The global prediction market opportunity exceeds **$50 billion annually**, yet existing platforms remain inaccessible to mainstream users. Hunch addresses three critical barriers:

1. **Complexity** of traditional interfaces
2. **Friction** in wallet management
3. **Regulatory uncertainty** through crypto-global distribution
